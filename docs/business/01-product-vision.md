# BookSmart Product Vision

## Vision

BookSmart is an AI-powered multi-business booking platform that enables customers to discover, compare, and book services from various businesses while providing business owners with a unified management system.

## Problem Statement

Many service businesses still manage appointments manually through phone calls, messaging applications, or spreadsheets. This often leads to double bookings, scheduling conflicts, poor customer experience, and inefficient business operations.

Customers also struggle to find suitable services based on their preferences, availability, location, and ratings.

## Target Users

### Customer

- Search businesses
- Book appointments
- Make online payments
- Receive AI recommendations
- Review completed services

### Business Owner

- Manage business information
- Manage employees and resources
- Manage bookings
- Manage services
- View reports and revenue

### System Administrator

- Manage users
- Verify businesses
- Handle reports
- Monitor the platform

## Product Goals

- Simplify appointment booking
- Support multiple business types
- Improve customer experience using AI recommendation
- Reduce scheduling conflicts
- Provide an easy-to-use management platform

## Core Features

- Authentication & Authorization
- Business Management
- Service Management
- Resource Management
- Appointment Booking
- Online Payment
- AI Recommendation
- Notifications
- Reviews & Ratings
- Dashboard & Analytics

## Out of Scope (Version 1.0)

- Loyalty Program
- Membership
- Live Chat
- Video Consultation
- Coupon System

## Success Metrics

- Users can book appointments successfully.
- Businesses can manage bookings efficiently.
- AI provides relevant recommendations.
- Payment transactions are completed successfully.
